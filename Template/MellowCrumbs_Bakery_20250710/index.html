OUR STORY

A Legacy of Baking Excellence

Since 1952, Mellow Crumbs has been dedicated to creating exceptional baked goods using time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients.

70+ Years

Baking Tradition

Our Heritage

Mellow Crumbs was founded in 1952 by Eleanor Winters, a passionate baker who emigrated from France with her family recipes. What started as a small neighborhood bakery has grown into a beloved local institution while retaining its old-world charm and commitment to quality.

Organic Ingredients

We source only the finest organic ingredients for our baked goods.

Traditional Methods

Each item is crafted using time-tested techniques passed down through generations.
