ABOUT ARCMOD

Founded in 2010, ArcMod is an architectural firm dedicated to creating spaces that balance functionality with aesthetic purity. Our work is deeply influenced by the principles of the Bauhaus movement, emphasizing form follows function, mass production, and the integration of art and technology.

We believe architecture should serve as a catalyst for human experience while respecting the surrounding environment. Our approach combines rigorous analysis with creative problem-solving to produce designs that stand the test of time.

Featured Project

THE CUBE RESIDENCES

A revolutionary housing complex that reimagines urban living through modular design and sustainable technology. The Cube Residences exemplifies our commitment to innovative solutions that maximize space and minimize environmental impact.

The project features 50 modular units arranged in a geometric grid, each with flexible interior spaces that adapt to the changing needs of modern living. The exterior facade incorporates photovoltaic panels and a rainwater harvesting system, making the complex energy-efficient and self-sufficient.
